Interstellar Lab

Interstellar Lab is a pioneering company focused on developing bio-regenerative habitats for space and Earth. Their mission is to create sustainable, closed-loop environments that support human life in extreme conditions, such as space missions and harsh terrestrial environments. By combining advanced engineering, AI-driven agriculture, and biomimicry, Interstellar Lab designs modular systems that recycle air, water, and nutrients to enable self-sufficient living, with applications ranging from space exploration to sustainable farming on Earth.

San Mateo County Sheriff's Office

The San Mateo County Sheriff's Office Investigations Bureau is responsible for conducting in-depth criminal investigations within the county. It specializes in solving complex cases, including homicide, fraud, cybercrime, narcotics, and other serious offenses. The bureau works closely with local, state, and federal agencies to gather evidence, analyze forensic data, and apprehend suspects. With a focus on public safety and justice, the Investigations Bureau plays a critical role in ensuring law enforcement integrity and protecting the community.
